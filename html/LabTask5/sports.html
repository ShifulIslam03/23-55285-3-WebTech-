Argentina Wins the 2022 FIFA World Cup

December 20, 2022

Argentina won the 2022 FIFA World Cup by defeating France in an unforgettable final held in Qatar on December 18, 2022. Lionel Messi scored twice and played a key role in the team's success. After a thrilling 3–3 draw, the match was decided by a penalty shootout, where Argentina won 4–2. Goalkeeper Emiliano Martínez made crucial saves to help secure the victory. This was Argentina’s third World Cup title and Lionel Messi’s first World Cup trophy, making it a historic moment for football fans worldwide.
